## 1. üìò Deep Theory: The 3-Step Structure

A professional description always follows this pattern. Do not jump straight to the numbers.

### Step 1: Einleitung (Introduction)

* What is the topic?
* What is the source?
* What is the timeframe?
* *Phrase:* "Die Grafik zeigt..." / "Das Thema der Grafik ist..."

### Step 2: Hauptteil (Main Part - The Trends)

* Do not read every number. Describe **Extremes** (High/Low) and **Changes** (Rising/Falling).
* *Phrase:* "An erster Stelle steht..." / "Die Zahlen sind stark gestiegen."

### Step 3: Fazit (Conclusion)

* Summarize the main finding in one sentence.
* *Phrase:* "Zusammenfassend kann man sagen, dass..."

## 2. ‚ö†Ô∏è Professional Insight: The Preposition Trap

This is where 90% of students fail. You must distinguish between **UM** and **AUF**.

* **Steigen AUF (Rise TO):** The new end result.
* *Example:* 100‚Ç¨ -> 110‚Ç¨. "Der Preis ist **auf** 110 Euro gestiegen."


* **Steigen UM (Rise BY):** The difference.
* *Example:* 100‚Ç¨ -> 110‚Ç¨. "Der Preis ist **um** 10 Euro (oder 10%) gestiegen."
