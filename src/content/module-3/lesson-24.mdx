## 1. üìò Deep Theory: The 90-Second Clock

You need a clear internal clock. Divide your presentation into 4 blocks:

1. **Einleitung (The Hook):** 15 Seconds.
* State topic + Why you chose it.


2. **Haupteil A (Description):** 30 Seconds.
* What happened? Who is the author? Where did you go?


3. **Haupteil B (Deep Dive):** 30 Seconds.
* Why was it special? How did it affect you? (Use Emotions).


4. **Schluss (The Landing):** 15 Seconds.
* Summary + Recommendation.



**The "Blackout" Protocol:**
If you forget a word in the middle: **Do not stop.** Paraphrase it.

* *Forgot "ambulance"?* -> "Das Auto, das kommt, wenn man verletzt ist."

## 5. üß† Logic & Grammar: "Indem" vs. "Sodass" (Consequence)

* **Indem (By doing X):** Describes the method.
* *"Der Autor erzeugt Spannung, **indem** er viele Geheimnisse einbaut."*


* **Sodass (So that):** Describes the result.
* *"Das Buch ist sehr einfach geschrieben, **sodass** man es schnell lesen kann."*
