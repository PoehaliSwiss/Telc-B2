## 1. üìò Deep Theory: The 4-Point Blueprint

In the exam, the prompt will usually ask you to address four things. Your letter should follow this exact paragraph structure:

1. **Einleitung (Introduction):** Reference the advertisement and state why you are writing.
2. **Situation (Reality vs. Expectation):** What was promised? What happened? (Use *Zwar...aber*).
3. **Folgen (Consequences):** How did this affect you? (Use *Nomen-Verb-Verbindungen*).
4. **Forderung (Demand):** What do you want? (Refund/Apology).
