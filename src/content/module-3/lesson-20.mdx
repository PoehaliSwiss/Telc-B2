## 1. üìò Deep Theory: The 3 Types of "Difficult" Partners

### Type A: The Steamroller (Der Dampfwalzer)

Talks non-stop, ignores you, decides everything alone.

* **Strategy:** **Interrupt politely but firmly.** You must get your speaking time!
* **Key Phrase:** *"Entschuldigung, wenn ich Sie unterbreche, aber..."*

### Type B: The Wall (Die Mauer)

Says only "Ja", "Nein", "Gut". Gives no ideas.

* **Strategy:** **Ask Open Questions (W-Fragen).** Do not ask Yes/No questions.
* **Key Phrase:** *"Wie genau stellen Sie sich das vor?"* (Not: "Ist das okay?")

### Type C: The Skeptic (Der Nein-Sager)

Rejects every idea: "No, too expensive." "No, boring."

* **Strategy:** **Put the ball in their court.** Ask them for a better idea.
* **Key Phrase:** *"Verstehe. Haben Sie denn einen besseren Vorschlag?"*
