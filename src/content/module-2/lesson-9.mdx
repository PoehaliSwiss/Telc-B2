## 2. ‚ö†Ô∏è Strategy: The "Aber" Pivot

In journalistic texts (B2/C1), authors structure their arguments like this:

1. **The Counter-Argument:** "Many people think X is good..." (*Zwar...*)
2. **The Pivot:** "...however/but..." (*...aber / allerdings / jedoch*)
3. **The Author's Opinion:** "...in reality, X is bad because..."

**Rule:** The author's true opinion is usually found **after** the connector (aber, doch, jedoch).
