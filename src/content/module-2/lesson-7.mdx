## 1. üìò Deep Theory: What are "Nomen-Verb-Verbindungen"?

At level B1, you use simple verbs. At B2, you often replace a simple verb with a **Noun + Functional Verb**.
The verb often loses its literal meaning and just serves to carry the grammar, while the noun carries the meaning.

* **B2 (NVV):** Wir m√ºssen **eine L√∂sung** f√ºr das Problem **finden**. (We must find a solution for the problem.)

**Why use them?**

1. **Precision:** They sound more formal and objective.
