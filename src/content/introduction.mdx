### The Roadmap: 4 Blocks to Success (30 Lessons)

The course is divided into four strategic blocks, each building upon the previous one.

#### **Block 1: The Architecture (Advanced Grammar)**

* **Focus:** Mastering the complex sentence structures that define academic and professional German.
* **Key Topics:**
* **The Connector System:** Moving beyond *und/aber* to *folglich, dennoch, einerseits/andererseits*.
* **The Passive Voice:** Essential for formal writing and describing processes.
* **Konjunktiv II:** The language of politeness and hypothesis.
* **Participles:** Using verbs as adjectives (*das weinende Kind* vs. *das verkaufte Auto*).
