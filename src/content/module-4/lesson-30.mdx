## 3. ‚öîÔ∏è During the Exam: Crisis Management

Things **will** go wrong. Expect it. Here is how to handle it.

### Crisis A: "I don't understand this Text!" (Reading)

* **Panic Reaction:** Re-reading the same sentence 5 times.
* **Pro Strategy:** Skip it. Answer the easy questions first. Often, the meaning becomes clear *after* you read the rest of the text. If you still don't know: **Guess B or C**. Never leave a blank answer.

### Crisis B: "The Audio is too fast!" (Listening)

* **Panic Reaction:** Trying to translate every word.
* **Pro Strategy:** Close your eyes. Focus **only** on the Nouns and Verbs. Ignore the small words. Listen for the *emotion* (Is the speaker angry? Happy? Skeptical?).

### Crisis C: "My Mind is Blank!" (Writing)
