## 4. üß† Deep Dive: Part 2 (The Interview Trap)

This is the hardest part. The interview lasts 5-7 minutes. You hear it **once**. There are 10 questions (5 True/False, 5 Multiple Choice).

**The "Anchor" Strategy:**
The questions follow the chronological order. You must know when the speaker moves from Question 7 to Question 8. Listen for the **Moderator's Signal Questions**.

* *Moderator:* "Kommen wir nun zum Thema Geld..." -> **Alert!** Moving to the next block of questions.
* *Moderator:* "Ein anderes Problem ist ja die Umwelt..." -> **Anchor** for the next question.
