## 1. ‚è≥ Deep Theory: The Time Budget

You must be your own timekeeper. Here is the optimal schedule for the 90 minutes.

* **Part 1 (Global Reading / Headlines):** 10 Minutes (Max!)
* **Part 2 (Detail Reading / Comments):** 25 Minutes (The hardest part)
* **Part 3 (Selective Reading / Ads or News):** 15 Minutes
* **Sprachbausteine 1 (Multiple Choice):** 10 Minutes
* **Sprachbausteine 2 (Blind Gap):** 10 Minutes
* **Buffer / Checking:** 20 Minutes

**Golden Rule:** If you don't know an answer after 2 minutes, **guess and move on**. Mark it to check later if you have time.

## 2. üëÅÔ∏è Technique: The "Scanner Eye" (Reading Part 1)

In **Part 1**, you match 5 headlines to 5 short texts. There are 10 headlines total (5 are distractors).
**Mistake:** Reading the whole text line by line.
**Strategy:** **Scan** for Keywords.

**Process:**

1. Read the **Headlines** first. Underline keywords (Nouns/Verbs).
2. Scan the **Texts** for synonyms of those keywords.
3. Match.
