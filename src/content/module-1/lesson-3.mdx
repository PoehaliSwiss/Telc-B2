## 1. üìò Deep Theory: The "Verb + Preposition" System

In English, you say "to wait **for**". In German, you say "warten **auf**".
At B2 level, memorizing the verb is not enough. You need the **Triad**:

1. **The Verb:** *sich freuen*

3. **The Case:** *Akkusativ* or *Dativ*

### The Logic of "Da-" and "Wo-" (Pronominaladverbien)
